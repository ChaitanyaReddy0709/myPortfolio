Project

Title: Design & Analysis of a 3-Arm Robot Using Different Materials

In this project, I led the design and analysis of a three-arm robot using CATIA V5 for 3D modeling and ANSYS for structural evaluation.

• The primary objective was to compare the performance of Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP), Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP), and Aluminum (AL) as potential materials for the robotic structure.

• The study involved creating detailed 3D models, applying real-world load conditions, and analyzing key mechanical properties such as stress distribution, deformation, and weight efficiency.

• The results provided valuable insights into material selection for lightweight yet durable robotic applications.

Tools Used: ‣ CATIA V5 ‣ Ansys
